Info Page

Clicking on each task under the checklist leads you to a tree representing your progress for each task.
Everytime you complete a task, check in by coloring in a leaf by tapping on the color and then a leaf.
The counter represents the number of days you have completed the task.
For every week you’ve checked in, you’ll receive a butterfly notification congratulating you on your hard work.
Coloring in all the leaves indicates that you’ve successfully completed the task for 28 days. This indicates that you are familiar with the task and have gained a new skill to aid you in your future art journey.
You’ll be rewarded with an alpaca for completing the tree and for making great progress towards your overall goal.
